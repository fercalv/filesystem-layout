## Scope

This file documents the intended structure of this personal filesystem.

It serves as a stable reference for organisation and long-term use.

---

## Root directories

00_SYSTEM  
Infrastructure files that support the filesystem itself.  
This includes templates, small scripts, configuration notes or setup-related material.  
No personal documents, projects or reference content should live here.

01_WORK  
Work-related material.

02_PROJECTS  
Personal or independent projects.

03_KNOWLEDGE  
Reference material and notes.

04_PERSONAL  
Personal documents and life-related files.

05_MEDIA  
Photos, videos, audio and other media files.

06_ARCHIVE  
Inactive or finished content.

99_INBOX  
Temporary intake for new or unsorted files.

---

## Naming convention

Files follow this format:

YYYY-MM-DD_description[_vN].ext

Examples:  
2026-01-04_meeting_notes.pdf  
2026-02-10_project_report_v2.docx
