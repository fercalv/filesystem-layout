## Scope

This file documents the intended structure of this work directory.

It exists as a reference for consistency and handover.

---

## Root directories

00_ADMIN  
Administrative and HR-related documents.

01_PROJECTS  
Active and ongoing work projects.

02_MEETINGS  
Meeting notes and related material.

03_REFERENCE  
Reference documentation and read-only material.

04_DELIVERABLES  
Final outputs and files shared externally.

99_INBOX  
Temporary intake for new or unsorted files.

ARCHIVE  
Inactive or closed content.

---

## Naming convention

Files follow this format:

YYYY-MM-DD_description[_vN].ext

Examples:  
2026-01-10_meeting_notes.docx  
2026-02-05_project_report_v2.pdf
